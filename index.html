OUR SERVICES

PROPERTY INSPECTION

+

Once an inspection is requested and scheduled, one of our removal experts will arrive on site to inspect the extent of any damage and determine the type of animal causing damage.

WILDLIFE EXCLUSION

+

This is a multi-step process starting with a property inspection to locate entry poin. Once all entry points are located, exclusion devices are installed to allow wildlife to safely exit.

PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE

+

Most homes are built to code by construction companies with little or no input from wildlife experts, leaving small openings that wildlife can use to gain entry into your property.

ANIMAL CARCASS REMOVAL

+

When nature takes its course, there may be unfavorable situations left behind. To help prevent the spread of parasites and disease, those carcasses must be disposed.

RODENT PREVENTION

+

Rodents can gain entry to a home by chewing holes or using crevices. After a thorough inspection is completed all crevices will be sealed to prevent future rodent activity.

LIVE TRAPPING

+

Trapping is our last line of defense and in some cases, the only option. In the instance that trapping is the only option available Contact Us: 973-572-6461

ATTIC ENVIROMENT CLEAN UP

+

Multiple types of nuisance wildlife invade attics and other spaces in your home, and each one of them presents their own set of problems with damage and contamination.

BIRD GUARDS

+

Bird guards are installed over bathroom exhaust fans and dryer vents to prevent nesting birds from clogging the vents with nesting material.

ATTIC FAN/VENT COVERS

+

Keep out racoons, squirrels, birds and many other nuisance wildlife by installing covers on all the ventilation exits on your home.

CHIMNEY CAPS

+

Chimneys offer warmth and shelter, which is why it can become a home to wildlife. Commonly, trapped wildlife can die inside the chimney and produce a dreadful odor in your home.

Rabies Warning

Rabies is a preventable viral disease most often transmitted through the bite of a rabid animal. The rabies virus infects the central nervous system of mammals, ultimately causing disease in the brain and death. Rabies can infect all warm-blooded mammals and is found in the saliva of rabid animals.
